Itzhak Bentov


Profile

Itzhak Bentov was an eccentric mystic, inventor and an author/researcher on adventurous subjects such as metaphysics, physics, biology and spirituality. His research has invited the attention of the CIA in the 60's and 70's. The CIA has utlized his findings in their reseach into psychic phenomena and universal cosmology. He was spearheading discussions about the human soul and consciousness by writing a book and appearing as a guest on an intellectual talk show. Unfortunately, Itzhak Bentov met a sudden end due to the crash of American Airlines flight 191 in 1979.


Notable Works

  • Stalking the Wild Pendulum - On the Mechanics of Consciousness
  • A Brief Tour of Higher Consciousness
  • (Referenced on) The CIA's Analysis and Assessment of Gateway Process
  • Invented the steerable heart catheter
